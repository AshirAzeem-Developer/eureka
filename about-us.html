About Us

Bentham Science is a science, technology, and medical publisher, providing academic researchers and industry professionals with the latest information in the diverse fields of science and technology. Our peer-reviewed scholarly journals and books (under the Bentham Books brand) have a growing readership of millions of researchers worldwide.

Originally incorporated in the Netherlands, Bentham Science is now a multinational company based in the UAE, with a strong presence in the USA, the Netherlands, Spain, India, and China. We also outsource publishing services to countries including the UK, China, and Pakistan.

Bentham Science currently publishes more than 130 journals in both electronic and print formats. Our journals cover a range of disciplines in pharmaceutical research and development, medical subspecialties, engineering, technology, and social sciences. The journals are indexed by recognized indexing agencies. Learn more about where our publications are indexed here: https://benthamscience.com/indexing-agencies

62 Bentham Science journals have received impact factor rankings in the annual Journal Citation Report 2025. Learn more: https://www.eurekaselect.com/press-release-detail/194

Bentham Science follows the double-blind peer-review procedure for submissions of all manuscripts to its journals, except for a selected number of patent journals where the double-blind review is followed.

All submitted research content is subject to an extensive peer review in consultation with members of the journal's editorial board and independent external reviewers (usually three reviewers). All manuscripts/chapters are assessed, and the decision is taken by the journals Editor-in-Chief after considering the comments of all the peer reviewers. The final decision is then conveyed to the author(s).

Since reviewers play an essential role in maintaining the integrity and accuracy of the research, Bentham Science has made its publications and review information available on Publons to acknowledge their role. Publons formally recognizes our peer reviewers by displaying a verified record of their peer review and editorial contributions to academic journals on its website. Learn more: https://publons.com/publisher/219/bentham-science-publishers

In addition to the wide range of scientific journals, Bentham Science has a book publishing program (Bentham Books), which publishes works on diverse topics. Our book catalog features more than 1000 titles, with a number of titles published annually. We continue to broaden our publishing scope by expanding our books and journals catalog on an annual basis to include works relevant to established and emerging disciplines. Learn more about Bentham Books here: https://benthambooks.com/.

Bentham Science offers authors a reliable publishing experience from the initial manuscript submission to final publication through our state-of-the-art journal management system. This makes the publication process smooth and timely. With a productive association of 10,000 eminent scientists on the editorial boards (including Nobel laureates as honorary advisors), Bentham Science provides high-quality peer review services to maintain its standards in publication.

In addition to publication opportunities for researchers, we also offer scholarly content licensing solutions through our online store, Eureka Select, to academic and corporate libraries. The online store also makes our publications available for individual readers and publishers. Visit EurekaSelect.
